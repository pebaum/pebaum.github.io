## Location 1: Home Village Termina
**Theme:** Fleeing your burning home
**Difficulty:** Tutorial (Target: 63% win rate)

### Card Distribution (19 cards)
- 2 Items (dummy: Old Boot, Older Boot)
- 2 Equipment (Gnarled Branch, Empty Prism)
- 5 Beasts (3×3HP, 2×5HP)
- 1 Hollow (7 HP)
- 1 Terror (5 HP or 1 Lux)
- 3 Pit (TN 3+, 2 HP per fail)
- 1 Snare (Cannot spend Lux)
- 1 Blessing (Heal 10 HP)
- 3 Caesura (Gain 1 Lux each)

**Expected:** 63% win rate, end with 10 HP
