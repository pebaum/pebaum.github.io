# The Council of Four — Bracket 4 (High Power) Commander Deck

## Commander
**The Council of Four** {3}{W}{U}
- *Whenever a player draws their second card during their turn, you draw a card.*
- *Whenever a player casts their second spell during their turn, you create a 2/2 white Knight creature token.*

---

## Deck Strategy — Bracket 4 Version

This is the **optimized** Council of Four build. We're maximizing:
1. **Fast mana** to deploy Council early
2. **Powerful stax** to slow opponents while we generate value
3. **Combo potential** as backup win conditions
4. **Premium interaction** including free counterspells

This deck aims to **control the table** while passively generating insurmountable value.

## Key Combos Explained

### Dramatic Scepter
1. Imprint Dramatic Reversal on Isochron Scepter
2. With 3+ mana from rocks, tap rocks → activate Scepter → untap rocks
3. Generate infinite mana
4. Draw deck with Staff of Domination or Blue Sun's Zenith yourself
5. Win with Thassa's Oracle or Lab Man

### Roaming Throne Synergy
- Name "Noble" for Roaming Throne
- Council triggers TWICE for each second card drawn
- Council triggers TWICE for each second spell cast
- With token doublers, each second spell = 4+ Knights

## Gameplay Lines

**Ideal Opening:**
- Turn 1: Land, Mana Crypt/Sol Ring
- Turn 2: Land, Council of Four + hold Fierce Guardianship
- Turn 3+: Generate 3+ cards per turn rotation, deploy stax

**Mid-Game:**
- Let opponents try to answer Council while you counter key threats
- Build token army passively
- Deploy draw doublers (Teferi's Ageless Insight)

**Closing:**
- Option A: Overwhelming token advantage + Cathars' Crusade
- Option B: Dramatic Scepter → infinite mana → win
- Option C: Draw into Approach of the Second Sun, cast twice
