HOW TO PLAY

  • Start: 10 hearts each, draw 5 cards
  • Turn: Both play 1 card face-up
  • Win type? Deal YOUR number as damage
  • Lose type? Take THEIR number as damage
  • Same type? Bigger number wins (damage = difference)
  • More hearts = reveal first
