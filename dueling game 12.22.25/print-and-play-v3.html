BLADE & HONOR

A Game of Steel and Deception

Setup

  • Each player: 15-card deck, 10 HP
  • Shuffle deck, draw 3 cards
  • See YOUR cards' true types
  • See only opponent card BACKS

Card Types

ATTACK > MANEUVER > DEFENSE > ATTACK

Round Flow (3 Clashes)

  • Clash 1: P1 leads, P2 responds
  • Clash 2: P2 leads, P1 responds
  • Clash 3: P1 leads, P2 responds
  • Resolve clashes in order: 1, 2, 3
  • Discard all 6 cards, draw 3 each
  • Next round: swap lead order

Combat

  • Winning type: Deal YOUR power as damage
  • Same type: Higher power wins, deals difference
  • Losing type: Take THEIR power as damage

Trick Cards

Each deck has 6 trick cards where the back doesn't match the true type!

  • Gray text = what opponent sees
  • Bottom label = true type
  • Use tricks to deceive!

Winning

  • Reduce opponent to 0 HP, OR
  • More HP when both decks empty

Deck Contents (15 cards)

  • 5 Attack backs: 3 Attack + 1 Defense + 1 Maneuver
  • 5 Defense backs: 3 Defense + 1 Attack + 1 Maneuver
  • 5 Maneuver backs: 3 Maneuver + 1 Attack + 1 Defense
