Poems & Songs

A collection of verses exploring the intersection of technology, nature, and human experience.

Digital Dreams

In circuits of thought and silicon streams,
Where data flows like midnight dreams,
We build our worlds with code and care,
Creating beauty from pixels bare.

Algorithmic Verses

Variables dance in memory space,
Functions call with recursive grace,
While loops eternal spin their tales,
And binary logic never fails.

Generative Haiku

Random seeds scatter
Across the digital garden—
Beauty emerges

Pixels paint the screen
Colors chosen by the code—
Art finds its own way

ASCII rivers flow
Through terminal windows wide—
Text becomes landscape

Songs of Systems

There's a rhythm in the server hum,
A melody in the code we run,
Each bug a note that's out of tune,
Each fix a harmony resumed.
