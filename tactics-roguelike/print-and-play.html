Setup

  • Shuffle all tiles face-down (‚ùì side up)
  • Arrange in a 5√ó5 grid
  • Place START tile face-up in one corner
  • Start with 10 HP and 0 coins
  • Place HP marker on 10, Coin marker on 0
  • Keep item cards (Key, Mushroom, Star) to the side

Your Turn

  • Move 1 tile orthogonally (up/down/left/right)
  • Flip the tile face-up if hidden
  • Resolve the tile effect
  • You cannot move diagonally
  • You cannot move through Blocked tiles

Combat (TO HIT System)

When you encounter a monster:

  • Roll 1d6 each round
  • If you roll ‚â• TO HIT number: You win!
  • If you roll below: Take damage, roll again
  • Keep rolling until you hit

Example: Koopa has TO HIT: 3+. You roll a 2, take 1 damage. Roll again, get 4, you win! Gain 1 coin.

Items

  • Key: Required to pass Door tile
  • Mushroom: Use anytime to heal 3 HP
  • Star: Block next damage taken OR auto-defeat any non-boss monster

When you get an item, take the matching item card and hold it. Discard when used.

Toad House (Shop)

  • Pay 3 coins: Heal 5 HP
  • Pay 5 coins: Gain Mushroom item

Health & Coins

  • Max HP: 10 (cannot exceed)
  • Max Coins: 10 (cannot exceed)
  • Move markers on counters to track
  • If HP reaches 0: Game Over!

Winning

  • Find the Key tile
  • Reach the Door tile
  • Discard Key to pass through Door

Bowser Jr is optional but gives 3 coins!

Tips

  • Save your Star for tough fights or emergencies
  • Visit Toad House when low on HP
  • Goombas are easy - fight them for coins!
  • Coins are precious - spend wisely
